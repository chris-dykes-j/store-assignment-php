About

Christopher Dykes

I'm a musician, part of the local music scene in Ottawa, instrument of choice is the Drums.
I currently play a variety of styles of music, including jazz, funk, rock, and latin.
I have had previous experience playing a wide variety of instruments, including the guitar, saxophone, clarinet, and piano.

Qualifications

  • Education in Music Stream at Carleton University.

  • Ability to play a variety of instruments.

  • Years of experience giving instruction.


Accomplishments

  1. Earned Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Carleton University

  2. Numerous performances in public concerts, and private venues.

  3. Released albums through Bandcamp.
